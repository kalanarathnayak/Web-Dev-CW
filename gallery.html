Mona Lisa

Mona Lisa

Artist - Leonardo da Vinci

Year - 1503


This painting features a picture of an elegantly dressed, seated woman, against an imaginary scenery. This is one of the most valuable and famous painting with the Guinness Wold Record.

Girl with Pearl Earring

Artist- Johannes Vermeer

Girl with Pearl Earring

Artist -Johannes Vermeer

Year - 1665


The girl in this picture is attracted by the viewers for her simplicity as she wears a gold coloured jacket and a matching turban which is blue and gold with Pearl earring. She is conveying a message with her facial expressions.

The Scream

Artist- Edvard Munch

The Scream

Artist - Edvard Munch

Year - 1893


This was painted as a result of a traumatic incident he faced while walking forward on a bridge leaving his two friends behind. The state of being anxious is expressed by the shocked face in this art.

The Son of Man

Artist- René Magritte

The Son of Man

Artist- René Magritte

Year - 1946


This self-portrait has the face of the man, wearing a coat in blue, covered by a green apple which expresses that everything we see hides another and from the peeking eyes of the man over the edge of the apple shows that we always have the urge to see what is hidden by what we see.

Self-Portrait with Grey Felt Hat

Artist- Vincent van Gogh

Self-Portrait with Grey Felt Hat

Artist- Vincent van Gogh

Year - 1887


This artwork is a self- portrait of himself with Grey Felt Hat. He focused on the dotted Pointillist technique and applied it in his own style. He has used long brushstrokes with complementary colours by applying orange and blue for the background, green and red for the eyes and beard.
